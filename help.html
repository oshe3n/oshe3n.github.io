Sticky Notes App - Help and Troubleshooting

Getting Started

Installing the App: Visit the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android) and search for "Sticky Notes App." Download and install the app on your device.

Creating Your First Sticky Note:

  1. Open the app.
  2. Tap the "+" or "Add" button to create a new sticky note.
  3. Type your note content.
  4. Optionally, use the color palette to change the note color.
  5. Tap "Save" to create your sticky note.

Common Issues and Solutions

Sticky Notes Not Syncing Across Devices:

  • Ensure you're using the same account on all devices.
  • Check your internet connection; syncing requires an active connection.
  • Try manually syncing by pulling down on the notes list.

App Crashes or Freezes:

  • Make sure your app is updated to the latest version.
  • Restart your device and relaunch the app.
  • If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall the app.
