Get Help - StickyNote

Get Started with StickyNote
First Time Using StickyNote?

Simply navigate to the Teams tab and search for the StickyNote app. Once found, click to install and you're ready to start creating notes!

Want to Delete a Note?

Select the note you want to delete and click on the 'Trash' icon. Confirm your decision in the pop-up window.

Scenarios and Troubleshooting
1. App notes not syncing?

Click the Sync from Cloud button to manually sync your notes.

2. StickyNote tab missing in Teams?

Ensure you've installed StickyNote correctly. If it's still missing, contact your Teams admin to ensure it's approved for use.

3. Notes appearing distorted on mobile?

Adjust the text size settings on your mobile device. If the issue remains, ensure you're using the official Teams mobile app.

4. Can't delete a note?

Ensure you have the necessary permissions to delete the note. If you're the note creator and still can't delete, try restarting the Teams app.

5. StickyNote app crashes frequently?

Update to the latest version of Teams and StickyNote. If the issue persists, try reinstalling the app.

6. Text formatting issues in notes?

Ensure you're using standard text formats. Avoid copying text directly from rich-text editors without pasting as plain text first.

7. Can't find a specific note?

Refresh the page, load the app again or sign back in to Teams and check again.

8. Notifications not working for StickyNote?

Currently the feature for StickyNote is not enabled.

9. Error when trying to install StickyNote?

Ensure you have the necessary permissions to install apps on Teams. If you do, try again after a few minutes or contact your IT department.

10. Notes not saving automatically?

Check your internet connection. If it's stable, try manually saving the note or restarting the app.

11. Can't access StickyNote on a new device?

Ensure you're logged into the correct Teams account. If you are, try reinstalling StickyNote on the new device.

12. StickyNote UI elements not loading properly?

Clear your Teams cache or try accessing Teams from a different browser or device.

13. Voice notes not recording?

We dont support voice notes at the moment reach out to us directly for any feature ask, contact information is given at the end.

14. Can't share StickyNote with a colleague?

For your own security we dont allow sharing notes between users, this feature is coming very soon in a secured way.

15. StickyNote not available in Teams store?

It might be restricted by your organization. Contact your Teams admin or IT department for clarification.

16. Error message "Failed to Load StickyNote"?

Try restarting Teams. If the issue persists, there might be server-side issues. Wait for a while and try again.

17. Can't switch between multiple StickyNote accounts?

Log out from your current account and then log in with the desired account. Ensure you're using the correct login credentials.

18. StickyNote not working after Teams update?

There might be compatibility issues. Check if there's an update available for StickyNote or contact the app's support team.

19. Lost notes after logging out?

Notes are tied to your Teams account. Ensure you're logged into the correct account. If you are, sync from the cloud to retrieve your notes.

20. StickyNote lagging or slow response?

Close other unused tabs or apps. If StickyNote is still slow, check if there's an update available or contact the app's support team.
